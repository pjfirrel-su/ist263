Patrick Firrell's Biography

My name is Patrick Firrell, and I am a Recording and Entertainment Industries Major at Syracuse University interested in digital design as it relates to artists and music companies.

I currently create social media content for WJPZ Z89 as well as my own podcast, DormTalk, utilizing Adobe programs such as Premiere Pro, InDesign, Photoshop, and Illustrator. I am also learning how to design and build websites using prototyping software like Adobe XD and Figma as well as HTML and CSS.
